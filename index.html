FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know before stepping into the arena.

What ages can participate?
Dodgeball leagues and drop-in sessions are for adults ages 18+. Birthday parties and space rentals are available for ages 10 and up. No participants under age 10.
What kind of dodgeballs do you use?
For adult league and drop-in play, we use S&S Gator Skin balls — the industry standard for competitive dodgeball. For kids' events and birthday parties, we provide softer balls for safe, comfortable play.
When are you opening?
We're targeting Summer 2026 (July) for our grand opening at Perinton Square Mall. Sign up below to be the first to know!
Where are you located?
Perinton Square Mall, 6720 Pittsford Palmyra Rd, Fairport, NY. Easy access and plenty of parking.
What's included with space rental?
Every rental includes access to the full 1,650 sq ft turf play area, music system, dodgeballs, gaga pit, and 4-square equipment. Perfect for team bonding, social gatherings, and private events.
Is this a kids' facility?
Fairport Dodgeball is primarily an adult-focused venue. We offer kids' birthday parties and space rentals for ages 10+, but our core programming — leagues and drop-in — is built for adults 18 and older.
Can I form a league team?
Absolutely! When you sign up below, let us know if you'd be willing to be a team captain and help form a team. We'll also help connect individual players to existing teams.
What league nights are available?
League play is planned for Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday nights. Tell us your preferred nights in the form below so we can plan accordingly.
